Today, the Regular Check-in Inbox drives cadenced proactive outreach to members based on assigned track. However, this is configurable to accommodate changes to any underlying variables, including:
Prioritization signal (e.g., P32 acuity, impactable opportunities)

What is the IOS?

The IOS is a score that integrates 5 opportunity types into a single weighted value (listed below).
Every member is assigned an IOS, which updates daily.
Member lists can be ranked by the aggregate IOS, as well as by individual opportunity types within the IOS.

IOS = Adjunct Revenue + TCOC Reduction Potential

Adjunct Revenue
Quality
BOI gaps
TCOC Reduction Potential
Care pathways and clinical programs

Documentation and System Loop Closure
Following a member interaction, RN CMs and CHPs document their care progress, which automatically updates members queued in the panel view for engagement
Product Build: Loop Closure
Loop closure is how Cityblock monitors that actions surfaced for care teams are completed. This includes both documentation support for care teams to record progress on identified member needs, as well as automatic system identification of completed work.

Loop closure ties together the panel view and the defragmented view. Work identified and completed during a comprehensive interaction impacts when a member will next be queued for engagement.
